Our Story

A Legacy Born in Angeles City

Founded in 2007, Wilcare Aqua began with a simple mission: to provide the highest standard of clean drinking water to our neighbors. What started as a small local station has grown into a trusted community institution.

We believe that water is the foundation of health. That is why we combine advanced 16-stage purification technology with the manual care and oversight that only a family-run business can provide.
