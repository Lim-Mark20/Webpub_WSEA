Our Story

Our Story: A Legacy of Care

From Family Values to Community Service

Established in 2007, Wilcare Aqua began with a vision to provide safe, affordable, and clean drinking water to the residents of Angeles City. For over 18 years, we have remained a local leader in water purification.

Our journey started with a small station and a big heart. Today, we utilize high-end multi-stage purification technology while maintaining the manual care and personal service that only a dedicated family business can offer.
